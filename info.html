Don't expect a lifestory

I am Shehab, an Engineering student at Ain Shams University, Faculty of Engineering, I probably made this website to get away from assignments and procrastinate, y'know, just another Wednesday.

This website is intended to be a portfolio of my work, and a place to share my thoughts and ideas, and maybe even some of my projects. I am not a professional web developer, so don't expect anything fancy, but I will try to make it as good as I can. I've started my GitHub account in 2023, better late than never, right? I originally started the GitHub to be some sort of a backup for my projects and assignments, but I've decided to make it public, and share my work with the world. I'm not sure if future me is going to be as active as I am now, but I'll try to keep it up.

What kind of items will you find here?

Random bullshit actually. Mostly projects and assignments, sometimes random facts and quotes, and maybe even some of my thoughts and ideas. If you're lucky enough you might even get rickrolled. Twice.

I would appreciate it if you could give me some feedback, and maybe even some advice, I'm always open to new ideas and suggestions, My github repositories are open for contributions and discussions, so feel free to go check them out, ask any questions and share your thoughts!

So in conclusion ...

The website is still under continuous development, expect bugs and glitches, and maybe even some broken links, I'm still learning. And I'll probably keep learning for the rest of my life, so don't expect this website to be perfect.
